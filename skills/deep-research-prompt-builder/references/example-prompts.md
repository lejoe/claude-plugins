# Research Prompt Examples

Complete example prompts demonstrating best practices across different research categories.

## Product Research Example

**User Topic:** "Best laptop for programming"

**Enhanced Prompt:**
```
# Research Objective
Identify and analyze the best laptops currently available for professional software development, with emphasis on performance, developer-specific features, and value.

# Context and Scope
- Purpose: Inform purchase decision for full-stack development work
- Boundaries: Currently available models (2024), $1000-$3000 range, US market
- Focus Areas: Build quality, performance, keyboard, display, battery life, Linux compatibility

# Research Requirements

## Investigation Depth
Primary questions:
1. Which laptops best serve professional developers in 2024?
2. How do top options compare on developer-critical features?
3. What are common issues developers report with popular models?

Secondary considerations:
- Future-proofing for next 3-4 years
- Ecosystem considerations (peripherals, support)

Explicitly exclude: Gaming-focused laptops, tablets, Chromebooks

## Evidence Standards
- Source types: Professional reviews, developer forums, benchmark sites
- Recency: Past 12 months for reviews, 6 months for prices
- Credibility: Established tech sites, verified developer experiences
- Citations: Include source and date for all claims

## Analysis Framework
- Performance benchmarks (compilation times, Docker, VMs)
- Developer ergonomics (keyboard, trackpad, ports)
- Display quality for long coding sessions
- Battery life under development workloads
- Linux/WSL compatibility and driver support
- Build quality and reliability reports
- Price-to-performance ratio

## Output Structure
### Required Sections:
1. Executive Summary (Top 3 recommendations with one-line reasoning)
2. Detailed Analysis of Top 5 Options
   - Specifications relevant to development
   - Pros/cons for developers specifically
   - Real developer experiences
   - Current pricing and availability
3. Comparison Matrix (key specs side-by-side)
4. Common Issues and Dealbreakers
5. Purchase Timing Considerations
6. Links to Current Best Prices

# Quality Instructions

## Reasoning Approach
- Prioritize real developer experiences over specs
- Consider both immediate needs and 3-year longevity
- Weight practical use over theoretical performance
- Account for ecosystem (dock compatibility, external monitors)

## Critical Evaluation
- Verify price information is current
- Note any widespread quality control issues
- Distinguish marketing claims from real performance
- Flag any models with recent refreshes coming
```

## Technical Research Example

**User Topic:** "Implementing micro-frontends"

**Enhanced Prompt:**
```
# Research Objective
Comprehensive analysis of micro-frontend architecture patterns, implementation strategies, and best practices for enterprise-scale applications.

# Context and Scope
- Purpose: Evaluate feasibility and approach for migrating monolithic frontend
- Boundaries: Focus on React/Vue ecosystems, enterprise B2B applications
- Focus Areas: Architecture patterns, tooling, team organization, migration strategies

# Research Requirements

## Investigation Depth
Primary questions:
1. What are proven micro-frontend architectural patterns?
2. Which tools and frameworks best support micro-frontend development?
3. How to migrate from monolith without disrupting development?

Secondary considerations:
- Team structure and ownership models
- Performance implications and optimization
- Testing strategies across micro-frontends

Explicitly exclude: Mobile app implementations, backend considerations

## Evidence Standards
- Source types: Technical documentation, case studies from major companies, open-source examples
- Recency: Past 2 years (rapidly evolving space)
- Credibility: Production implementations, major tech companies, framework maintainers
- Citations: Link to repos, articles, documentation

## Analysis Framework
- Architecture patterns (Build-time vs Runtime integration)
- Communication strategies between micro-frontends
- Shared dependencies and version management
- Development workflow and tooling
- Deployment and orchestration approaches
- Performance impact and mitigation
- Real-world case studies with outcomes

## Output Structure
### Required Sections:
1. Executive Summary (Recommended approach with rationale)
2. Architecture Patterns Deep-Dive
   - Pattern description and use cases
   - Implementation complexity
   - Trade-offs and gotchas
3. Tooling Ecosystem Analysis
   - Module Federation, Single-spa, Qiankun comparison
   - Build tools and deployment platforms
4. Migration Strategy Blueprint
   - Phases and milestones
   - Risk mitigation approaches
   - Team preparation needs
5. Code Examples and Reference Implementations
6. Decision Matrix for Pattern Selection

# Quality Instructions

## Reasoning Approach
- Start with problem space before solutions
- Consider organizational maturity alongside technical factors
- Balance innovation with proven practices
- Think in terms of evolutionary architecture

## Critical Evaluation
- Distinguish hype from production-ready solutions
- Note complexity costs vs benefits
- Highlight where micro-frontends may be overkill
- Include failure stories and lessons learned
```

## Academic Research Example

**User Topic:** "Impact of sleep on cognitive performance"

**Enhanced Prompt:**
```
# Research Objective
Synthesize current scientific understanding of sleep's impact on cognitive performance, including mechanisms, optimal patterns, and practical applications.

# Context and Scope
- Purpose: Comprehensive literature review for evidence-based recommendations
- Boundaries: Human studies, past 10 years, healthy adult populations
- Focus Areas: Memory, attention, decision-making, creative problem-solving

# Research Requirements

## Investigation Depth
Primary questions:
1. What are the established mechanisms linking sleep to cognitive performance?
2. How do different sleep parameters affect specific cognitive domains?
3. What interventions effectively optimize sleep for cognitive enhancement?

Secondary considerations:
- Individual variations and genetic factors
- Age-related changes in sleep-cognition relationship
- Interaction with other lifestyle factors

Explicitly exclude: Clinical populations, sleep disorders, pharmaceutical interventions

## Evidence Standards
- Source types: Peer-reviewed journals, meta-analyses prioritized
- Recency: Past 10 years, emphasis on last 5 years
- Credibility: Impact factor >3, established research institutions
- Citations: Full citations with DOI links

## Analysis Framework
- Neurobiological mechanisms (consolidation, clearance, restoration)
- Sleep parameters analysis:
  - Duration effects (dose-response curves)
  - Sleep quality metrics
  - Sleep timing and chronotypes
  - Nap effectiveness
- Cognitive domain-specific impacts:
  - Memory (declarative, procedural, emotional)
  - Executive function
  - Attention and vigilance
  - Creative problem-solving
- Practical interventions evaluation
- Methodological quality assessment

## Output Structure
### Required Sections:
1. Executive Summary (Key findings and practical implications)
2. Mechanistic Understanding
   - Neurobiological processes
   - Molecular and cellular mechanisms
3. Evidence Synthesis by Cognitive Domain
   - Effect sizes and consistency
   - Moderating factors
4. Optimal Sleep Parameters
   - Evidence-based recommendations
   - Individual variation considerations
5. Intervention Effectiveness
   - Behavioral interventions
   - Environmental modifications
6. Research Gaps and Future Directions
7. Practical Guidelines Translation

# Quality Instructions

## Reasoning Approach
- Prioritize meta-analyses and systematic reviews
- Note effect sizes, not just statistical significance
- Consider ecological validity of lab findings
- Integrate across levels of analysis (molecular to behavioral)

## Critical Evaluation
- Assess study quality and potential biases
- Note replication status of key findings
- Distinguish correlation from causation
- Acknowledge contradictory findings
- Consider publication bias possibilities
```

## Business Research Example

**User Topic:** "AI adoption in healthcare market"

**Enhanced Prompt:**
```
# Research Objective
Analyze the current state and trajectory of AI adoption in healthcare, including market dynamics, key players, barriers, and growth opportunities.

# Context and Scope
- Purpose: Strategic analysis for potential market entry
- Boundaries: US market primarily, 2022-2024 data, B2B focus
- Focus Areas: Diagnostic AI, clinical decision support, operational efficiency

# Research Requirements

## Investigation Depth
Primary questions:
1. What is current market size and growth trajectory for healthcare AI?
2. Which use cases show highest adoption and ROI?
3. What are primary barriers and accelerators to adoption?

Secondary considerations:
- Regulatory landscape and compliance requirements
- Competitive dynamics and consolidation trends
- Funding patterns and investor sentiment

Explicitly exclude: Consumer health apps, wellness AI, medical devices

## Evidence Standards
- Source types: Market research reports, regulatory filings, industry analyses
- Recency: Past 18 months for market data
- Credibility: Established research firms, CDC/FDA data, peer-reviewed health economics
- Citations: Include source, date, and methodology where relevant

## Analysis Framework
- Market sizing and segmentation
  - By application area
  - By provider type
  - By geography
- Adoption curve analysis
  - Current penetration rates
  - Growth projections
  - Leading vs lagging segments
- Competitive landscape mapping
  - Key players by segment
  - Business models
  - Strategic partnerships
- Value chain analysis
- Regulatory environment assessment
- Barrier/enabler analysis
- Investment and M&A activity

## Output Structure
### Required Sections:
1. Executive Summary (Market opportunity and key insights)
2. Market Overview
   - Size, growth, segmentation
   - Adoption metrics and trends
3. Use Case Analysis
   - High-value applications
   - ROI evidence
   - Implementation challenges
4. Competitive Landscape
   - Major players and positioning
   - Emerging companies to watch
   - Partnership ecosystems
5. Regulatory and Compliance Factors
6. Investment Thesis
   - Opportunities and risks
   - Entry strategies
   - Timeline considerations
7. 18-Month Outlook

# Quality Instructions

## Reasoning Approach
- Triangulate data across multiple sources
- Distinguish hype from actual deployment
- Consider both technical and organizational readiness
- Balance optimistic projections with realistic constraints

## Critical Evaluation
- Verify market size estimates methodology
- Note potential conflicts of interest in sources
- Separate pilot projects from scaled deployments
- Account for healthcare's unique adoption challenges
```
